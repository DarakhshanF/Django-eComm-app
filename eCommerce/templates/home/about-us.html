Welcome to SurreySolars

Surrey Solars, headquartered in the vibrant town of Guildford and closely associated with the prestigious University of Surrey, stands at the forefront of sustainable energy solutions. Our mission is unequivocal: to pioneer the path towards achieving net zero emissions in Guildford by 2050. With a deep-rooted commitment to environmental stewardship, we specialize in providing cutting-edge solar technology and products tailored to meet the evolving needs of residential, commercial, and industrial clients alike. At Surrey Solars, we recognize that the transition to renewable energy sources is not just a necessity but a moral imperative, and we take pride in offering innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solutions to propel our community towards a brighter, cleaner future.

Embedded within our ethos is a dedication to excellence, sustainability, and community engagement. By harnessing the power of solar energy, we empower individuals and businesses to take control of their energy consumption while significantly reducing their carbon footprint. Collaborating closely with local authorities, businesses, and homeowners, we strive to foster a culture of environmental responsibility and innovation. Through meticulous research, development, and implementation, we aim to redefine the landscape of energy production and consumption, one solar panel at a time. At Surrey Solars, we envision a world where sustainability is not just a choice but a way of life, and we are committed to leading the charge towards a cleaner, greener tomorrow.
